Spotify Strategy and Innovation

Spotify is one of the largest music streaming services in the world. If we take a look at the industry, the products only fulfill one purpose: streaming music.

But music is much more than sheer consuming. Music is felt. It offers us a gate to another dimension, where we can escape reality.

Our songs accompany us through life and the music we listen to describes us.
People who like similar music will probably like each other.

Since music is more than just mere consumption, Spotify should offer more than just streaming.


Spotify: Music connects.



Extending the Premium offering by massively improving analytics and the platform aspect of the app. (Following, sharing, find new people,...) To become the largest music-based "social media" worldwide and gain the edge over all competitors.
