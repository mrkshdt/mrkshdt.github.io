Hi there!

How's it going? My name is Markus and I'm a final year bachelors student at the Technical University Kaiserslautern, where I'm studying Computer Science with a concentration in Software Engineering.

I'm highly interested in the impact Computer Science can generate in an enterprise and society. Since December 2018, I'm working as a Data Science Working Student analyzing large sets of data from the SEC, learning a lot about Machine Learning use cases in the financial industry and sustainability. I'm volunteering at the local Stock Exchange club where I got elected as Vice President and give a Data Science seminar for students from different departments. Additionally, I'm volunteering at the founders club, holding an interactive lecture about Future Authoring. Occasionally, I participate in Hackathons to solve highly complicated problems with innovative products. So far, I have participated in 4 Hackathons, from which I won 2.
